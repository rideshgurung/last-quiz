Round 4: Individual Round

“See it, think it, answer it — let your eyes lead your mind!”

Rules & Format

  • Each house will get 4 questions in total.
  • Every question must be answered within a 20-second time limit.
  • Once a number is chosen, that question cannot be changed or skipped.
  • No question can be repeated or chosen by another team.
  • If a team fails to answer within the time, the question will be considered missed.
  • There will be no passing of questions to other houses.
  • Points will be awarded based on the correctness of the answer.
  • Teams must maintain discipline and avoid discussing during other teams’ turns.
